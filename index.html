Approach & Expertise

The frameworks and tools I use to build great products

1. Discovery & Strategy

This phase involves briefing with stakeholders to gather requirements, followed by in-depth research (market analysis, competitor review, user interviews, field observations, data analysis) to thoroughly understand the 'WHY' and define the problem space.

This leads to shaping the initial product strategy.

2. Design & Delivery

Here, we brainstorm & create solutions through rapid prototyping, wire-framing, and collaborative discussions with designers and developers.

This feeds into working within an Agile Squad, managing the Backlog, and collaborating closely with the QA Team for quality delivery.

3. Launch & Iteration

This final stage includes the launch of the product, followed by continuous measurement of its success against defined metrics.

Decision Making then occurs by balancing user insights, business OKRs, and effort tradeoffs to inform future iterations and product evolution.
